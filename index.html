Welcome to Carnivorous Cultivars


I have only had my traps about a year now, but they have divided incredibly fast. A little too fast for me to have space for in my current situation. I really only have space for about 6 or 7 traps, and I also have a couple house plants that I squeeze onto a window sill. I love plants and can't wait to get more, but I just don't have the space for all of them right now.

My dad came up with the idea to grow more and sell them if I don't have room. I also enjoy coding, so here we are. If you have any complaints or suggestions about my website feel free to share them with me. I will update the type of traps available as I get more, but for now I only have 4 cultivars and the 'typical' Venus Fly Trap (I guess you can call it the default trap).

This website is still a work in progress. I haven't decided how I want to handle the back-end, so once I figure that out I'll finish the shop of the website.
